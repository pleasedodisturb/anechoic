# Doist Handbook - Comprehensive Summary

**Source:** [doist.com/how-we-work](https://doist.com/how-we-work)  
**Company:** Doist (Todoist, Twist) - 50+ employees, 30+ countries  
**Key Innovation:** 95% async communication, Twist platform  
**Format:** Summarized into core bulletpoints per section

---

## Company Philosophy

### Async by Default
- **95% of communication is asynchronous**
- Real-time (Slack, calls) used only when truly needed
- Built Twist specifically to enable async work
- Proves async works for product development at scale
- Teams span 10+ timezones without issue

### Core Values
- **Independence** - Work without constant oversight
- **Mastery** - Continuous learning and improvement
- **Ambition** - High standards, sustainable pace
- **Communication** - Clear, thoughtful, written
- **Balance** - Work is part of life, not all of it

---

## Twist: Async Communication Platform

### Why Twist Over Slack
| Slack | Twist |
|-------|-------|
| Real-time pressure | Async by design |
| FOMO on channels | Miss nothing important |
| Endless scroll | Threaded conversations |
| Presence indicators | No "green dot" pressure |
| Ephemeral | Searchable archive |

### Twist Structure
- **Channels** for topics, not teams
- **Threads** keep conversations focused
- **No presence indicators** - No online/offline pressure
- **Inbox approach** - Like email, check when ready
- **Full history** - New members see everything

---

## Communication Norms

### 24-Hour Response Standard
- **Respond within 24 hours** to direct mentions
- Not instant - but reliable
- Enables deep work blocks
- Sets clear expectations
- Prevents anxiety from waiting

### Writing Standards
- **Complete thoughts** - Don't fragment messages
- **Context included** - Assume reader has none
- **Action clear** - What do you need?
- **Deadline stated** - When do you need it?
- **Thread replies** - Keep conversations together

### When Async Isn't Enough
- Sensitive feedback conversations
- Urgent production issues
- Complex negotiations
- Relationship building (occasional)
- Onboarding first days

## DO (Doist Objectives) System

### Structure
- **Quarterly objectives** company-wide
- **Squads** form around objectives
- **Cross-functional teams** - Not silos
- **Clear accountability** - Owner for each objective
- **Progress updates** - Async, weekly

### Squad Model
- Temporary teams for specific objectives
- Include all needed skills
- High autonomy
- Dissolve when objective complete
- Prevents organizational calcification

### Tracking Progress
- Weekly async updates in Twist
- No status meetings
- Visual dashboards
- Blockers surfaced immediately
- Celebrate completions publicly

## Work-Life Boundaries

### No Presence Pressure
- **No online indicators** in Twist
- **No expectation of instant response**
- **Flexible hours** within reason
- **Time off protected** - Really offline
- **Burnout prevention** - Sustainable pace

### Working Hours
- Suggested overlap windows (optional)
- Individual schedules respected
- No penalties for different hours
- Core collaboration windows per team
- Async fills the gaps

### Time Off
- Unlimited vacation (with minimums encouraged)
- No work during time off
- Coverage planned in advance
- Documentation enables handoffs
- Return without inbox nightmare

## Hiring & Onboarding

### What They Look For
- **Async communication skills** - Strong writing
- **Self-direction** - Works without supervision
- **Remote experience** - Or demonstrated capability
- **Values alignment** - Independence, mastery
- **Long-term thinking** - Not job hoppers

### Interview Process
- Mostly async (written exercises)
- Time-delayed responses expected
- Focus on work quality, not interview performance
- Cultural alignment critical
- Trial period before full commitment

### Onboarding
- **Comprehensive documentation** - Self-service
- **Buddy system** - Dedicated guide
- **Gradual immersion** - Don't overwhelm
- **Quick wins** - Build confidence early
- **Feedback loops** - Regular check-ins initially

## Meeting Philosophy

### Meetings as Last Resort
- **Async first** - Can this be a Twist thread?
- **Pre-work required** - No unprepared meetings
- **Agenda mandatory** - Know why we're meeting
- **Time-boxed** - End early if possible
- **Recorded** - For async participants

### Types of Meetings (Rare)
| Meeting Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------------|-----------|---------|
| 1:1s | Weekly/bi-weekly | Career, relationship |
| Team syncs | Monthly or less | Alignment, connection |
| All-hands | Quarterly | Company updates |
| Workshops | As needed | Complex collaboration |

### No Regular Standups
- Replaced by async check-ins
- Twist threads for status
- No daily synchronization required
- Updates when meaningful, not scheduled

## Documentation Culture

### Everything Documented
- Decisions in Twist threads (searchable)
- Processes in internal wiki
- Knowledge base for FAQs
- Onboarding guides comprehensive
- Nothing lives only in someone's head

### Documentation Standards
- Write for future reader
- Include context and reasoning
- Update when things change
- Link related documents
- Date and attribute
