# START HERE - Remote Communication Quick Start

**Time to read:** 20 minutes  
**Time to first win:** 1 week

---

## Why This Matters

Every Slack message, Jira comment, email, and meeting that seems insignificant in a day's scope **shapes culture**. This guide helps you build async-first communication habits that compound over time.

---

## The 30-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Understand the Core Principle (5 min)

> **"Async communication is not a tool limitation—it's a cultural value."**
> 
> Companies that excel (GitLab, Basecamp, Doist) build systems designed for distributed work, not replicas of office environments.

**The shift:** Instead of "when can we meet?", ask "how can I communicate this so anyone can respond when they're ready?"

### Step 2: Choose Your First Practice (5 min)

Pick ONE to start with:

| Practice | Best For | Effort | Impact |
|----------|----------|--------|--------|
| **Weekly Async Update** | Status visibility | Low | High |
| **Decision Document** | Clear decisions | Medium | High |
| **Channel Purpose Clarity** | Reducing noise | Low | Medium |
| **Meeting Decision Tree** | Fewer meetings | Low | High |

**Recommended first:** Weekly Async Update (Basecamp-style)

### Step 3: Create Your Template (10 min)

#### Weekly Async Update Template

```markdown
# [Program Name] - Week of [Date]

## 🎯 This Week's Focus
- [Main objective 1]
- [Main objective 2]

## ✅ Completed
- [Achievement 1]
- [Achievement 2]

## 🔄 In Progress
- [Work item 1] - [% complete or status]
- [Work item 2] - [% complete or status]

## 🚧 Blockers / Risks
- [Blocker 1] - [What's needed to unblock]

## 📅 Next Week
- [Planned work 1]
- [Planned work 2]

## ❓ Questions / Decisions Needed
- [Question for stakeholder X]

---
*Posted async - respond in thread by [day/time]*
```

### Step 4: Introduce to Team (10 min)

**Message template:**

> "I'm experimenting with async updates to reduce status meetings and give everyone more focus time. Starting this Friday, I'll post a weekly update in [channel]. Please respond in thread with questions or feedback. Let's try for 2 weeks and evaluate."

## Success Metrics (Week 4)

Track these to measure impact:

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Status meetings reduced | 30-40% fewer |
| Async response rate | >80% within 24h |
| Decision documentation | 100% of major decisions |
| Team satisfaction | Survey baseline + improvement |
| Focus time | +2-3 hours/week per person |

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### ❌ Don't: Go async-only overnight
**Do:** Start with ONE practice, prove value, expand

### ❌ Don't: Expect instant adoption
**Do:** Give it 2-3 weeks, iterate based on feedback

### ❌ Don't: Use async for urgent/sensitive topics
**Do:** Know when sync is right (see [TPM Communication Framework](./frameworks/tpm-communication-framework.md))

### ❌ Don't: Create documentation nobody reads
**Do:** Keep it scannable, update regularly, link from decisions
