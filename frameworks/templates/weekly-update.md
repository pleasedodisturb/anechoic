## Tips for Effective Updates

1. **Keep it scannable** - Busy people skim first
2. **Lead with what matters** - Executive summary is most read
3. **Be specific about help needed** - Generic asks get ignored
4. **Include trends** - Not just snapshots
5. **Tag people** - Direct asks get responses
6. **Set response deadline** - Creates accountability
7. **Link to details** - Keep update concise, link for depth
